Kylie Jenner Inspired High Ponytail



Sometimes all you need to make a statement is a simple, sleek ponytail. Simple, and fierce, this look is perfect to pair with embellished outfits at classy events, and can also be worn more casually. Here we show you how to recreate the sleek, high ponytail Kendall Jenner wore to the Met Gala, so you can feel red carpet ready.

Step 1: Wash your hair
Exactly like how we need to apply foundation to our faces to get the perfect makeup look, you also need to wash your hair with the perfect products to get the look you desire. For this look, the TRESemmé Keratin Smooth Shampoo and Conditioner is perfect to use, as it is specifically formulated to give you that smooth, sleek look.

Step 2:
Gather your hair up into a high ponytail. Twist a piece of hair from the ponytail around the elastic band. Secure with a bobby pin. To add volume to the hair at the top of your head, use a pin-tailed comb to plump out some of the hair.

Step 3:
To get rid of flyaways and to keep the style intact, use TRESemmé Salon Finish Extra Hold Hair Spray. Use it at a close range, about 10cm away from your hair. Using your curling wand, lightly go over the flyaways.

Step 4:
There you have it, a Kendall Jenner inspired high ponytail, perfect for any occasion.
