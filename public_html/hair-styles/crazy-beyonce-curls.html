Crazy Béyonce Curls

Don’t know what to do with your hair this silly season? Worry not, Queen Bey has us covered.



Beyoncé has been known to rock huge, wild curls that fills us all with hair envy. Fun, playful, and fierce, this hairstyle is perfect for casual, outdoor occasions and for when you want to make a statement without having to say a word. Follow our step-by-step guide to achieving this look.

Step 1: Prep your hair
You can use the Dove Oxygen Moisture Shampoo and Conditioner. This range is perfect for fine hair as iit gives volume and a light, airy feel to your hair that’s perfect for this hairstyle.

Step 2: Divide and conquer
Divide your hair in half, running your comb from ear to ear. Then section off the top and divide the back half into small sections in order to get tight curls. Remember, the smaller the pieces of hair you use, the tighter the curl will be.

Step 3: Curl away
Wrap a small piece of hair from a bottom section around a small curling wand, clasp it, and roll it close to the root. Hold in place for a few seconds and release. After each section is complete, use TRESemmé Salon Finish Extra Hold Hair Spray to seal the curl so it won’t fall loose. Remember to hold the can 20-30cm away from the hair when you’re spraying. After locking the curls in place with the hairspray, use a fine-toothed comb to tease the roots. This adds more volume to your hair. Repeat all steps until all your hair is done

Step 4: The finishing touches To achieve more voluminous, bouncy curls use your hands to slightly loosen the curls and tease the hair. This also helps to create a natural finish. Loosen and tease the hair in an upward direction (instead of going downward) and enjoy your crazy Scurls!
