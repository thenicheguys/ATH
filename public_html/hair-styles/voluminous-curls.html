Voluminous Curls



Big hair is definitely making a comeback, 2016 is all about volume and a few curls to spruce up the look! Here are a few simple steps to get you on the top of S.A’s fashion game with amazing voluminous curls.

Step 1: Prep your hair
The key to any hairstyle is how you prep your hair. For volume, the TRESemmé Beauty-full volume range is a must. It’s a reverse system, so you condition your hair first, then shampoo it, to ensure that your hair is voluminous, and not weighed down - without compromising on softness and smoothness. Next in line is the -TRESemmé Beauty- Full Volume Maximiser and the TRESemmé Beauty- Full Volume Mousse, to ensure your hair stays big and bouncy all day long.

Step 2: Section your hair and curl
Divide hair into four sections and secure them separately.Choose a piece of hair from a section in the back, that is closest to the nape of your neck. Use a medium to large curling wand and wrap your hair around it, curling away from your face. Leave for twenty seconds, then release. Secure each curl to help it keep its shape. Repeat this process throughout the rest of your hair. Remember to curl away from your face.

Step 3:
Spray a generous amount of TRESemmé Salon Finish Firm Hold onto the hair, holding it 20-30cm away from your hair.

Step 4:
Allow your hair to cool for the curls to set. Once cool, release all the curls. If you want structured curls, feel free to end here. For loose, natural curls, shake the curls out slightly and gently run your fingers through your hair.
