Twisted Braid Upstyle



Braids are back, and they’re hitting the fashion scene all over in a big way! It’s understandable really: they’re pretty and versatile for all occasions and lengths of hair. With us going into Summer, braids are also the perfect hairstyle go-to, as they keep the hair off our necks and out of our faces, just one more way to beat the heat.

Step 1: Prep your hair
We suggest you wash your hair with the Dove Nourishing Oil Care Shampoo and Conditioner to nourish your hair and keep away the frizz. This is perfect for this hairstyle as you want it smooth and easy to work with.

Step 2:
Create a middle path in your hair, parting your hair all the way to the nape of your neck. Tie up one section to keep it separate and take a medium-sized piece from the front of your hair and divide it into 2. Twist both pieces towards your face. Remember not to twist too much or it will look too structured. We’re aiming for the romantic look, so it’s okay if it’s a bit messy. Then you can pull the piece that is close to your face over the other piece and then drop that piece. Pick up another piece and twist towards your face. Repeat until you reach the end of your hair. Tie off with an elastic.

Step 3: You’ll want to repeat these steps in the other side. If you want to make your hairstyle more playful and messy, gently pull on the hair, from the bottom of each braid, going up.

Step 4: Laying down the final touches. Secure each braid around the back of your head, going to the opposite side. Pin the second braid crossing over the first, so they are not just lying side by side. Tuck in the ends of the braids that are sticking out and secure the with bobby pins. We present to you, your beautiful twisted braid up style!
