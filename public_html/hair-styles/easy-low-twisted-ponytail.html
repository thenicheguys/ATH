Easy Low Twisted Ponytail



Spring has sprung, birds are singing and we are still so hair obssessed! So of course we had to come up with perfect Spring hairstyles for you, and this is one of them! It’s so simple, and perfect for any casual occasion, like lunch with the girls, retail therapy, or a picnic in the park.

Step 1: Prep your hair.
This hairstyle requires a lot of tugging, so adding a bit of strength to your hair is a need. We recommend the TRESemmé Platinum range as this will strengthen and protect your hair.

Step 2:
Make a parting in your hair (side or middle) and separate your hair from the parting to your ears, on both sides. Tie the hair at the back in a low ponytail and plump out the hair going into the ponytail to add volume.

Step 3:
On the opposite side of your head, repeat step 2, and repeat again with the two small sections in front (don’t divide these two sections), and secure one of the sections in the front to the side of your head. Divide the other section in two, and twist one piece around your finger. When it’s twisted, tug on it to make it a bit loose and messy. Pin it to the back of your head, just above the ponytail. Don’t worry about the ends of the twisted sections sticking out, we’ll see to that later.

Step 4:
Wrap the ends that are sticking out around the elastic band and secure underneath the pony. This is to hide the band, giving the style a prettier look. To make sure the hairstyle stays in place all day, use TRESemmé Salon Finish Extra Hold Hair Spray. Hold the can 20-30cm away from your hair. Now you’re Spring ready with your new gorgeous look!
