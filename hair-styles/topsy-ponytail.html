‘Topsy’ Ponytail



Spring is here, flowers are blooming, and hair is on our minds! We came up with some perfect Spring hairstyles for you, and this is one of them! It’s so easy to do, yet it looks so sophisticated and pretty, it’s really versatile and wearable for so many occasions.

Step 1: Wash your hair
The step step in any hairstyling routing is prepping your hair. This hairstyle requires a lot of pulling into many little ponytails, so adding a bit of strength to your hair is a must. We recommend the TRESemmé Platinum range as this will strengthen and protect your hair.

Step 2:
Section off a piece of your hair on the top of your head and tie it into a ponytail. Tie it a bit to the side (off-centre). Make a hole in the hair above the ponytail and pull your ponytail through the hole. Tighten it slightly and pull on the edges to adjust to look as you want it.

Step 3:
Section off another piece of hair just below the first piece and repeat both steps, ensuring it is also tied off-centre, on the opposite side as the first ponytail. Starting to see a pretty design unfolding? Repeat this process once again.

Step 4:
To make sure the hairstyle stays in place all day, use TRESemmé Salon Finish Extra Hold Hair Spray. Hold the can 20-30cm away from your hair.

Step 5:
For a spring-perfect look, add flowers to your hair by just [sticking] them in amongst the ponytails, wherever you think it will look good.

Step 6:
This hairstyle is so versatile, you can create a beautiful upstyle simply by twisting the hair at the, tucking it in and securing it with bobby pins. Absolutely simple, but it looks like you’ve put in s much effort!
